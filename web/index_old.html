About Kora:

Kora is a Catalan girl who has been gaining some recognition in the National scene in th last months. The release of her last EP is only the beging... On her computer, Kora produces songs with groovy baselines, dancing saxophones and she complements it all with a unique voice. She has sung in several languages in the past (Catalan, Spanish, Italian) but Drive, it the first time she sings in English.
